True IMAX

The Bob Bullock in Austin is the only full-size IMAX screen in the city — but it's digital-only. True IMAX means a full-size screen with a 70mm film projector, the highest quality format a film can be shown in. There are only two of those in Texas, and neither is in Austin.

  • Bob Bullock IMAX — Austin, TXFull-size screen, digital laser only — no 70mm film
  • Cinemark Dallas — Dallas, TX
  • AMC Rivercenter 9 — San Antonio, TX
Austin — Premium & Arthouse
  • Alamo Drafthouse — All Austin Locations 4K HDR / Dolby Atmos screens only — everything else is filtered out
  • Violet Crown Cinema Premium independent, 4K laser
Austin — Cinephile Programming
  • Paramount Theatre Summer Classic Film Series, special engagements
  • Austin Film Society Cinema Curated repertory, filmmaker events, member screenings
  • Hyperreal Film Club Rotating events, cult and rare programming
