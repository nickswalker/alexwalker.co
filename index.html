Bio

Alex Walker is a Filmmaker and Image Consultant based in Austin, Texas specializing in Cinematography and Color Correction. Alex has shot many international and regional ad campaigns, along with several documentaries, music videos, and feature films. His work for the feature film “No One Will Know” won the award for Best Cinematography at the Sunset Int’l FF in 2012. Alex’s list of credits includes work with companies such as HGTV, Hewlett Packard, Nissan, and VICE, as well as several well known Republican and Democratic political candidates.
