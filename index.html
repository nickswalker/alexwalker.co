Colorist

Bio

Alex is a cinematographer and colorist based in Austin, Texas and has shot many acclaimed international and regional ad campaigns, documentaries, and feature films. His work on the feature film “No One Will Know” won Best Cinematography at the Sunset International Film Festival. With 15 years in all aspects of the business, Alex also has a flourishing post production and colorist service.
