Colorist



Bio

Based in Austin, Texas, Alex is a cinematographer and colorist. He has brought his talents to a wide range of projects, from feature films to ad campaigns, documentaries, and TV shows.

Alex recently completed work on House of Abraham, directed by Lisa Belcher and starring Natasha Henstridge, Lukas Hassel, and Lin Shaye, which is set for a theatrical release. He also served as cinematographer and colorist for Texas Cult House, directed by Julia Barnett and starring Ryan Cooper and Adrianne Palicki, which premiered at Austin Film Festival 2024 and will soon be available on streaming platforms. Among his other recent projects is A Christmas In New Hope, directed by Julia Barnett and starring Katrina Bowden, Ryan Cooper, and Adrianne Palicki, which also had a theatrical release and is now streaming.

In addition to his cinematography work, Alex specializes in color grading and finishing, overseeing the delivery of films to distributors. He also collects screen-used and replica movie props, with a focus on sci-fi.
